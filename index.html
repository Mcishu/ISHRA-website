Our Story

Born from love,
stitched with soul.

ISHRA was born in the lanes of Chandni Chowk — from two best friends who spent hours picking the most beautiful ethnic pieces and dreaming of sharing them with the world.

We are Ishika & Shruti — and we started ISHRA because we believe every woman deserves to feel rooted, radiant, and real in what she wears. Every piece in our collection is handpicked by us, personally. No shortcuts. No compromise.

From printed kurtis to coord sets to festive drops — ISHRA is your ethnic wardrobe, curated with ishq. 💘

How to Order

01
Browse & Pick Your Favourite
Scroll through our Instagram @ishra.ethnic or WhatsApp catalogue and find the piece you're in ishq with 🌸
02
DM or WhatsApp Us
Tell us which piece you want and your size. We'll confirm availability and share all details instantly 💬
03
Pay via UPI
100% advance via UPI (PhonePe / GPay / Paytm). We'll send you a confirmation the moment payment is received 💳
04
We Pack with Love & Ship!
Your order is packed in our signature red potli with tissue & thank you card, and dispatched within 24 hours 🎀
